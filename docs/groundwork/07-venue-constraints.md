## Locked Constraints (PhiMiSci)
- **Target length:** 5,000–6,000 words (excl. references)
- **Abstract:** ≤ 200 words
- **Format:** PDF for submission; anonymized for double-blind review
- **AI disclosure:** Required in manuscript
- **Licensing:** CC-BY default
- **Source:** https://philosophymindscience.org/index.php/phimisci/about/submissions
